"Ke a leboga!" – Thank you!

Chapter 1 · FREE
Dumelang! 👋
Greetings and simple introductions
SetswanaEnglishSay it like…
DumelaHello (1 person)Doo-meh-lah
DumelangHello (many)Doo-meh-lung
Le kae?How are you?Leh kah-eh?
Re tengWe/I am fineReh ten-g
Tsamaya sentleGo well (goodbye)Tsah-mah-yah sen-tleh
Sala sentleStay well (goodbye)Sah-lah sen-tleh
Go siameOkay / GoodbyeGo seh-ah-meh
Ke a lebogaThank youKeh ah leh-boh-gah
✍️ Fill in the Blanks

1. ___________! (Hello to one friend)

2. Le kae? → ___________. (I am fine)

3. Someone is leaving → ___________!

4. Thank you → ___________!
